1. Overall Layout
    Header: Fixed at the top, containing the logo on the left, a navigation menu (Dashboard, Applications, Leases, Payments, Maintenance, Settings), and a user profile icon on the right.
    Sidebar: Positioned on the left, containing navigation options relevant to the current section (Rent Collection, Rent Reminders, Payment History, Renters Insurance, Renters ACH, Renters Check).
    Content Area: Main area displaying the primary content, titled at the top with a clear heading.
2. Typography
    Font Family: Use a clean, sans-serif font like Arial, Helvetica, or a similar web-safe font.
    Font Sizes:
        Header: 24px, bold
        Subheaders: 18px, bold
        Body text: 14px, regular
        Sidebar text: 16px, regular
3. Colors
    Primary Color: Black for text (#000000)
    Secondary Color: Grey for secondary text (#666666)
    Accent Color: Blue for buttons and highlights (#007BFF)
    Background Color: White (#FFFFFF)
        Status Indicators:
        Paid: Green (#28A745)
        Due: Red (#DC3545)
4. Buttons and Links
    Primary Buttons: Blue background with white text (#007BFF)
    Hover: Darker blue background (#0056b3)
    Secondary Buttons: Light grey background with black text (#F8F9FA)
    Hover: Darker grey background (#E2E6EA)
5. Tables
    Header Row:
        Background: Light grey (#F8F9FA)
        Text: Black (#000000)
        Font Size: 14px, bold
    Body Rows:
        Background: White (#FFFFFF) for even rows, light grey (#F8F9FA) for odd rows
        Text: Black (#000000)
    Font Size: 14px, regular
6. Icons and Images
    Profile Icon: Circular, positioned at the top right.
    Status Indicators: Circular or rectangular labels next to text, color-coded according to status.
7. Spacing and Alignment
    Margins and Padding:
    Header: 20px padding
    Sidebar: 15px padding, 10px margin between items
    Content Area: 20px padding
Alignment:
    Text: Left-aligned for lists and tables, center-aligned for headings.
    Buttons and Icons: Centered vertically within their containers.
8. Forms and Input Fields
    Labels: 14px, bold, black (#000000)
    Input Fields: 14px, regular, black (#000000), light grey border (#CCCCCC)
    Focus: Blue border (#007BFF)
